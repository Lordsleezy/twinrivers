Legacy

Built on Trust. Backed by Experience.

For more than 40 years, Twin Rivers Fence has helped Northern California homeowners, ranch owners, and businesses protect and improve their properties.

Long before online reviews and social media, our reputation was built the old-fashioned way - through honest work, quality craftsmanship, and customers recommending us to friends, family, and neighbors.

Over the decades we've watched communities grow, families expand, and businesses thrive. We've installed fences around family homes, secured commercial properties, built custom gates, and helped property owners protect what matters most.

While materials and techniques continue to evolve, our values remain unchanged:

Do the job right. Treat people fairly. Stand behind your work.
