Common fence problems we fix

Leaning or soft posts

Soil settlement, poor footing, or rot at grade. We reset or replace posts and reconnect rails so the line stands true again.

Broken boards and panels

Wind, impact, and age take out pickets and vinyl sections. Matching material and hardware keeps the repair from looking patched.

Gate and hardware failures

Sagging walk gates, sticky latches, and driveway gate alignment issues that leave yards insecure.

Storm and tree damage

Fallen limbs and saturated ground can take down whole runs. We stabilize unsafe sections first, then rebuild cleanly.

Fence repair FAQs

Can a leaning fence be repaired or does it need replacement?

Many leaning sections can be repaired by resetting posts or rebuilding a short run. Widespread rot usually means replacement is the better long-term value.

Do you repair wood, vinyl, and chain link?

Yes — wood privacy and ranch fences, vinyl systems, chain link, and gates tied into those fences.
