Why Customers Choose Us

Built on Craft, Communication, and Local Experience

Twin Rivers Fence is a family-owned contractor with more than four decades of fencing experience across the Sacramento Valley, Sierra foothills, and Gold Country. Property owners choose us when they want straight answers, durable materials, and crews who understand Northern California soil, slopes, HOAs, and weather.

Wood Fencing

Cedar and redwood privacy fences planned for your lot—true post lines, solid footings, and finishes that hold up through hot summers and wet winters. We build backyard enclosures, side-yard screens, and custom board-on-board layouts.

Vinyl Fencing

Low-maintenance vinyl panels for homeowners who want a clean look without annual staining. We install privacy heights, decorative tops, and layouts that work with existing landscaping and access points.

Chain Link Fencing

Practical perimeter fencing for pets, equipment yards, and commercial sites. Galvanized and coated options with secure gates, tensioned fabric, and posts set for long-term use.

Gates

Single walk gates, double drive gates, and custom access solutions matched to your fence line. We plan for daily use, security, and the clearances you need for trailers, RVs, and equipment.

Residential Fencing

Backyard privacy, pool enclosures, pet runs, and property-line fences for homes throughout Sacramento, Elk Grove, Roseville, Rocklin, Folsom, and Grass Valley. We walk the lot, explain options, and quote the footage honestly.

Commercial Fencing

Security perimeters, storage yards, and industrial sites that need dependable chain link, access control, and repair response. Twin Rivers Fence handles commercial scope with the same accountability we bring to residential jobs.

Northern California Experience

From valley heat to foothill rock and mud season, we plan installs around real site conditions—not textbook assumptions. That local knowledge shows up in post depth, drainage, and material choices that last.

Family-Owned Operation

Twin Rivers LLC is licensed California contractor #1089233. You work with a team that answers the phone, shows up when scheduled, and stands behind the work. That consistency is why customers return and refer neighbors.
